Scan a specific port range: 
rustscan -a 192.168.1.1 --range 1-1000
Scan multiple IPs: 
rustscan -a 192.168.1.1,192.168.1.5
Scan a subnet (CIDR): 
rustscan -a 192.168.1.0/24
Speed/Batch Tuning: If RustScan is missing ports due to system limitations, you can adjust the batch size (-b) and timeout (-T):bash
rustscan -a 192.168.1.1 -b 500 -T 1500
